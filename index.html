Our Story

Building trust through excellence in construction

Arsala Builder Private Limited is a leading construction company based in Kasaragod, India, known for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With a team of 70+ dedicated professionals, we've successfully delivered over 30 projects, earning the trust of 50+ satisfied clients.

Our Leadership

Iqbal Nalupurappattil

Founder & Managing Director

With over 15 years of experience in contracting and interior design, Iqbal brings extensive expertise in project management and client relations. His previous role as manager of an exporting company and 15 years of experience in the UAE as a PRO and secretary to the Sheikh of Dubai have shaped his visionary leadership.

Nassir Malingal

Co-Founder & Director

Nassir's expertise in construction and interior design was honed through his work on prestigious projects like the Dubai Mall and Emirates Post. His attention to detail and commitment to quality ensure every project meets the highest standards.
